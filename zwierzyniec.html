Zwierzyniec Browar Polski is a local, family brewery focused on different kind of beers. The recipe passed from father to son makes their products unique on the market.

Type of project: Branding, Packacking Design

Light Side & Dark Side

Beers presented on the mockups are divided into two groups: dark and light side, which corresponds with the type of beer they represent. For the dark beers cold colours (blue and purple) were used, and a more warmer tones were utilisied in the presentation of the light ones.

Each bottle features different animal representing type of flavour that can be found inside the bottle. The images were taken from British Library and transformed into vector art using Illustrator. This choice of design shows a perfect blend of old graphics and fresh packacking.
