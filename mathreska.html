Mathreska is a streetwear brand taking its inspiration from the roots, changing the folklore and giving it a second life.

Type of project: Branding, Illustration

Project Outline

Created by young people for young people, Mathreska aims to create modern, durable and good looking clothes that are inspired by slavic folklore. It tries to mix old and new, and the effect is astonishing.

Main colours of the presented collection are red and yellow. The sharp and fresh tone of red shows the rebellious nature of young people, while adding the yellow gives it the appropriate punch, enhancing the look by giving it more depth.
