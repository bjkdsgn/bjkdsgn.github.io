Petrichor is a fictional TV Show telling the story of a young girl who were drowned by priests in Vatican and resurrected as Hail Mary. It was inspired by Paulo Sorrentino's series called Young Pope. Petrichor means "scent of rain"

Type of project: Photography

Photoshoot

The stylization and make up done in the studio were made so that the actress would look like a statue resembling Hail Mary. With sad eyebrows, long eyelashes and fake tears made using a simple glue resulted in a stunning result.

Moody lighting, as well as blue and red filters used on the lamps in the studio helped establishing the mood and colour scheme of the show. That design appears on a cover of the NME magazine created especially for Petrichor.
