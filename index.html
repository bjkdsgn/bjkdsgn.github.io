Virtual Fitting Room

What is a virtual fitting room?

Virtual fitting room is an online shopping companion that gives customers ability to see clothes in 3D and customize their colours.

On the left, you can choose which item of clothing to customize. You can hide an unwanted item by clicking the eye icon.

On the right, you can change the background image to see how your outfits would look in different situations

You can rotate, zoom and drag the model.
